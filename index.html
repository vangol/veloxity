What is the price of a LockerPower cell phone charging station?

LockerPower offers various pricing for our plug and play cell phone charging stations:
  • You can rent a LockerPower station for any event or venue. The actual cost is determined by the duration of the rental agreement contact us to receive more information. We offer short-term event rentals (1-5 days) and long term rentals (6 months minimum).
  • You can purchase a LockerPower station anytime – contact us for a custom quote based on your budget and needs.

How many devices, phones, tablets, can charge at once?

Each charging station has 4 or 6 individual lockers, meaning it can charge 4 or 6 devices at once. The charging stations accommodate charging for 98% of the current smartphone market including all Apple iPhones, Android devices, Sony, Samsung, HTC, LG, Nokia and Blackberry phones.

How many LockerPower stations do I need for my event or venue?

According to usage statistics we have collected, we recommend (1) LockerPower charging station per 500 people at your event or venue.

What goes on the LCD display screen of each phone charging station?

TEach LockerPower charging station comes with a 19 inch LCD touchscreen that is fully customizable. The LCD screen will play media such as MP4 videos or images of any format. The LCD media can be changed by inserting a flash drive to the back of the charging station or by remotely accessing it.

What are the installation requirements and logisitcs?

There are no venue modifications necessary for installation unless you are mounting the charging station onto the wall. Upon receiving the charging station, you’ll merely have to screw on the base. The charging stations simply plug into a standard 3 prong power outlet.

Are LockerPower charging stations covered under warranty?

Each LockerPower charging station comes with a sixteen (16) month hardware warranty and a lifetime software warranty.
